## 2024-06-17 - Keyboard Accessibility focus-visible\n**Learning:** Interactive elements such as custom `<button>` and `<Link>` elements, particularly those hidden behind hover states, need manual application of `focus-visible` utility classes to remain accessible and visible to keyboard navigation.\n**Action:** Add `focus-visible:outline-none focus-visible:ring-2 focus-visible:ring-ring focus-visible:ring-offset-2 focus-visible:ring-offset-background focus-visible:opacity-100` to custom interactive elements.
